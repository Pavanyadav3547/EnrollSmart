EnrollSmart: Ideal for smart admissions counselors

Capture leads smartly with the power of AI

EnrollSmart is loved by counselors because it captures leads from all online and offline channels and displays them at one place. Gone are the days of duplicate leads with its smart standardized data-capturing fields.

EnrollSmart provides counselors with the ability to track leads on the basis of email engagement, last conversation, links clicked, etc. It also empowers them with deep insights into the intent of leads to personalize the enrollment efforts.
