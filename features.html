Lead Management

EnrollSmart allows you to capture leads from various platforms and assign them to counsellors. You can prioritize lead follow-ups as well as automate follow-up alerts. Several methods of lead nurturing is also available at your disposal. These include drip marketing campaigns and bulk Email/SMS. Furthermore, you can record calls, track lead behaviour, tag high-intent leads and much more.

Marketing Management

EnrollSmart aids you to create a marketing plan best suited for your organization. It helps you set annual enrolment targets and plan your marketing calendar accordingly. Furthermore, you can allocate channel-wise marketing budgets. You also don’t need to tediously rummage through multiple documents to compare Year-On-Year results.

Campaign Management

With EnrollSmart, you can create, monitor and measure the outcomes of all campaigns running across different channels through a single dashboard. EnrollSmart allows you to execute your marketing strategies to perfection and develop a sales direction for optimum ROI. You can streamline processes and automate many tasks in order to make enrolment effortless.

Predictive Analytics

EnrollSmart makes use of a variety of statistical techniques—from predictive modelling to machine learning. The algorithm captures relationships among various factors to help you analyze records and make enrolment predictions. It allows your institution to anticipate outcomes and behaviours based upon data and not assumptions.

Cloud Telephony

EnrollSmart is enabled with cloud telephony that moves your organisation’s phone system to the cloud. It is the smartest way to manage calls without compromising on quality and cost. It helps you to set up call centres with zero investments on infrastructure and allows a streamlined, controlled and systematic manner of communication.

ROI Metric

EnrollSmart has an inbuilt Return on Investment (ROI) metric that can be used to evaluate the returns on a marketing campaign or compare the efficiency of a number of different marketing campaigns. In short, it provides you a direct and easy-to-understand way of measuring profitability of your marketing spends.

From admission enquiry to enrollment,manage leads in a smarter way!
